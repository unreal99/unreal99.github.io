Air Conditioning, Energy Costs, and Your Health

Your AC unit has a powerful impact on two areas: your power bill and your own health.  Through years of training and specialization, we’ve acquired deep knowledge about both.

  1. Energy efficiency.  When nighttime temperatures climb over 100 degrees, your air conditioning system is going to be working very hard.  The smallest increases in energy efficiency will turn into big savings in peak use months.  Our technicians are trained to offer tips and advice that will lower your power bill, but don’t be afraid to ask for more.  All of our guys are experts and can save you plenty of money over time.
  2. Health.  The quality of the air you breathe while asleep is more important than you might now.  Most of us breathe through the mouth while sleeping, where our bodies own air filters are less prominent.  We’ve learned a lot about allergies, respiratory issues, and how air quality affects our lives.

Continuous Training

When the time comes that your air conditioning system stops working, it is usually when you need it the most. In Las Vegas this means it is an emergency.

In Las Vegas, air condition is not a luxury……..it is a necessity. If your air conditioning system fails during the summer, Cool Air Now knows it is an emergency. From the moment one of our service coordinators answer the phone you will feel that your situation is important to us, and you are being treated with the utmost professionalism through the entire process of getting your air conditioner repaired and restoring your families comfort.

Our managers and trainers are among the most qualified in the industry. A Cool Air Now repair technician under goes continual rigorous training to stay at the cutting edge of today’s ever changing industry. Just in the last 5 years the HVAC industry has under gone 2 major redesigns; one for the R22 phase out and the other for the minimum efficiency federal mandate.

Any HVAC technician that didn’t have access to continual training would now not be qualified to work on todays HVAC equipment. These are the questions you should be asking yourself:

  • Is there any protection for ME?
  • Will I be able to determine if a technician is or isn’t qualified?
  • What happens when a under qualified technician shows up to MY home?

Well, you may have heard of similar stories in the automotive industry. The mechanic makes an uneducated guess, looks you in the eye and tells you this is the problem. Then you pay to correct the problem only for him to come back with another problem, while charging you for his next guess. This process will continue until the under qualified technician eventually guesses his way to the actual problem after charging you for his inability to make a first time proper diagnosis. This is the best case scenario. The risk of an under qualified repairman causing catastrophic damage to your system is high. Things like using the wrong refrigerant or charging to improper specifications can cause irreparable damage.

Quality Parts

As you can well imagine, there are hundreds of different manufactures of HVAC parts. Customers don’t have the time or ability to find out and ensure they are receiving quality parts. Because of this, many air conditioning companies will just purchase and resell the cheapest parts available.

At Cool Air Now we make sure the parts our customers receive are 100% tested during and after manufacturing. Our manufactures perform quality inspections and testing to ensure functionality and reliability. We know that quality parts require things like, proper raw material, winding integrity, continuity, resistance, dimension, bearing design, assembly processes, proper facility and air quality when it comes to electronics. We believe our customers trust that we are looking out for their best interest. And they are right!
